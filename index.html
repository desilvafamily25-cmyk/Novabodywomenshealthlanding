You Are Not Alone

Does Any of This Sound Familiar?

These are not signs that you are "just stressed" or "getting older." They are recognised symptoms of perimenopause and menopause — and they are treatable.

🌡️

Hot Flushes & Night Sweats

Sudden waves of heat, drenching sweats, disrupted sleep

🧠

Brain Fog

Difficulty concentrating, forgetting words, feeling "not yourself"

💭

Mood Changes

Anxiety, irritability, low mood — often with no clear reason

🌙

Sleep Problems

Waking at 2–4am, difficulty falling asleep, never feeling rested

⚖️

Weight Changes

Weight creeping up, especially around the middle — despite no diet change

💪

Fatigue & Joint Pain

Persistent exhaustion, aching joints, low motivation and energy

📅

Irregular Periods

Cycles becoming unpredictable, heavier, lighter, or skipping

❤️

Low Libido

Reduced interest in intimacy, vaginal dryness, discomfort

💓

Racing Heart & Headaches

Palpitations, new or worsening headaches, sensory sensitivity

If your doctor has told you it's "just stress" or "part of getting older" — you deserve a second opinion. These are medically recognised symptoms. They can be investigated, assessed, and treated.

— Dr Premila Hewage, MBBS FRACGP · Founder, NovaBody Medical

Our Services

What Dr Hewage Offers You

This is not a generic telehealth appointment. Every consultation is focused specifically on your symptoms, your history, and your goals.

💊

HRT / MHT Assessment & Prescribing

Evidence-based menopausal hormone therapy — assessed for your individual health profile, risk factors, and preferences. Current formulations, properly monitored.

🩺

Perimenopause Assessment

Perimenopause can start years before your last period. Dr Hewage identifies where you are in the transition and builds a plan for each stage.

🌿

Non-Hormonal Options

Not every woman wants or can use HRT. Dr Hewage discusses all evidence-based non-hormonal alternatives — no pressure in any direction.

🦴

Bone & Heart Health

Oestrogen decline affects bone density and cardiovascular risk. Long-term health planning is part of every menopause consultation.

🔄

Follow-Up & Monitoring

Your care does not stop at the prescription. Follow-up appointments are built into your plan to assess your response and adjust treatment as needed.

🏥

Contraceptive Counselling

You can still be fertile in perimenopause. Dr Hewage provides contraceptive guidance appropriate for the transition period.
